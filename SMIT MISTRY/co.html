Recipes

  • Khandvi : Cook a smooth batter of gram flour and buttermilk with spices, spread thin, roll, and temper with mustard seeds and green chilies.
  • Dhokla – Steam a spiced gram flour batter with Eno, then top with a mustard-seed tempering.
  • Aloo Sabzi (Potato Curry) Sauté boiled potatoes with mustard, cumin, and Indian spices for a quick dry curry.
  • TheplaMake a soft dough with wheat flour, fenugreek leaves, and spices, roll thin, and cook on a griddle with oil.
  • Undhiyu A mixed vegetable curry cooked with spiced masala, fenugreek dumplings, and lots of oil in a slow-cooked method.
  • Handvo A savory baked lentil and rice cake made with vegetables and tempered with sesame seeds and mustard.
  • Sev Tameta nu Shaak Cook tomatoes with Indian spices and top with crispy sev just before serving.
